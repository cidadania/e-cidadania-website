Full-featured platform

e-cidadania is the first effort in trying to make a participative process combining online and in person participation. From the first debates to the proposals and votings, everything is done from the platform, without having to go from one platform to another.

Ordered debate system

e-cidadania includes a new debate system to leave the old linear discussion systems, it's time to draw conclusions!.

Free/Libre Software

With all the letters. All the platform, from the source code, including the design, documentation, extra tools, etc. its licensed under GPLv3 to guarantee its freedom forever.
