Mailing lists

e-cidadania mailing lists are places where people openly discuss about the platform or gives help to users who need it. There are some generic lists where the official language is english, in which people discuss about e-cidadania development and gives help to other users, but there are also mailing lists in your language. If you already searched and didn't find it, it can be that there are a very small user base in your language and because of that the mailing list wasn't created.

    English (official)

  • ecidadania-dev@freelists.org
  • ecidadania-users@freelists.org

    • Spanish

  • ecidadania-es@freelists.org

    • Galician

  • ecidadania-gl@freelists.org

How do I join a mailing list?

To join a mailing list, you must send an empty email with the subject "subscribe" (without the quotes) to the same address as the mailing list but adding "-request" at the end of the name.

For example, if you want to join the development mailing list you should send an email to: ecidadania-dev-request@freelists.org.

After that, you should receive an email telling you that you're subscribed to the list. From that moment you'll start receiving mail of the mailing list. If you feel like sending an email to the list you just have to send it to the regular list address (in the example: ecidadania-dev@freelists.org)

iRC

IRC channels are always a good option to ask questions. Currently we have four channels on the Freenode (irc.freenode.org) network.

#ecidadania
Channel for general discussion and help.
#ecidadania-dev
Channel for development related matters.
#ecidadania-es
Spanish language help channel.
#ecidadania-gl
Galician language help channel.
