Mailing lists and iRC

Mailing lists

e-cidadania mailing lists are places where people openly discuss about the platform or gives help to users who need it. There are some generic lists where the official language is english, in which people discuss about e-cidadania development and gives help to other users, but there are also mailing lists in your language. If you already searched and didn't find it, it can be that there are a very small user base in your language and because of that the mailing list wasn't created.

    English (official)

  • ecidadania-dev@freelists.org
  • ecidadania-users@freelists.org

    • Spanish

  • ecidadania-es@freelists.org

    • Galician

  • ecidadania-gl@freelists.org

iRC

IRC channels are always a good option to ask questions. Currently we have three channels on the Freenode network.

  • #ecidadania Channels for discussions and help.
  • #ecidadania-dev Development channel for e-cidadania
  • #ecidadania-es (spanish) Spanish help channel
